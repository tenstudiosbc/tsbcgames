Who We Are

Born from Community

TSBC Games is an indie game development team born from TenStudios Broadcasting Community — a non-profit community founded on October 6, 2014.

We create gacha titles, sprawling open-world adventures, and deeply story-driven RPGs. Every project we make is shaped by the voices of our community, because great stories need great listeners.

Our philosophy is simple: put heart into every pixel, every line of dialogue, every mechanic — and always keep the door open to the people who play our games.
