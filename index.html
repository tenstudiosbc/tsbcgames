Who We Are

Born from Community

TSBC Games is an indie game development team born from TenStudios Broadcasting Community — a non-profit community founded on October 6, 2014.

We create gacha titles, sprawling open-world adventures, and deeply story-driven RPGs. Every project we make is shaped by the voices of our community, because great stories need great listeners.

Our philosophy is simple: put heart into every pixel, every line of dialogue, every mechanic — and always keep the door open to the people who play our games.

SSIA Vanguard Chronicles

Windows Android — Coming Soon
🖥 Windows · RPG Maker VX Ace 📱 Android · RPG Maker MV
Download on Itch.io Bluesky Join Discord
In SSIA: Vanguard Chronicles, take on the role of a former SSIA special agent stripped of your power and purpose after a devastating ambush. But fate has more in store. After months of recovery, a mysterious lead calls you back to the SSIA and your destiny. Shadows rise, and your choices will shape the future of the realm. Features • Tactical 2D Turn-Based RPG with Elemental Counter Strategy • Elemental Counter System • Branching Narrative and Quests • Expanding World – Future Chapters Planned • Party Manager — up to 16 teams of 4 members each • Mail System • Combat Simulations • Voice-Acted Cutscenes • Voice-Acted Basic, Skill & Ultimate Attacks • Shaders via RGGS3 Scripting — no image overlays
